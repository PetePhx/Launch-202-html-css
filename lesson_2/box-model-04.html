4. Given the code below, what is the minimum width and height (in pixels) that the div needs to be to entirely contain the article element (including its margins)?

`div` and `article` have both visual formatting models of border-box.

The inner element here (`article`) has the inline-block display property.

For inline-block elements, the height property is taken into account.

article height: 300 + 20 + 10 = 330px

article width: 500 + 19 + 11 = 530px

To these div borders and paddings are added to arrive at the div box size:

div height: 332px

div width: 532px

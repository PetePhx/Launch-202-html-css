7. Challenge: Given our solution to the previous question, what will happen if we put the article tags on separate lines?
Try to figure out the answer without peeking. Why do you think this is?

Answer:

The browser seems to add an empty space in between the two `article` elements when the two are separated by an empty space or a newline character. Changing the article width propoerty to 49% allows for the empty space to be added in the parent element `section`.

For inline block elements, the blank space/new-line between elements is transofmed to a single white space, hence causing a shift in spacing.
